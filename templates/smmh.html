Understanding the Data

Each question is rated on a scale from 1 to 5, where 1 indicates 'Not at all' and 5 indicates 'Very much'.

Data Key:

  • Activity Interest Variability: How frequently does your interest in daily activities fluctuate?
  • Anxiety: How much are you bothered by worries?
  • Concentration: Do you find it difficult to concentrate on things?
  • Depression: How often do you feel depressed or down?
  • Distraction: How often do you get distracted by social media when you are busy doing something?
  • Restlessness: Do you feel restless if you haven't used social media in a while?
  • Self Compassion: How often do you compare yourself to other successful people through the use of social media?
  • Post Sentiment: Following comparisons on social media, how do you generally feel about these comparisons?
  • Sleeplessness: How often do you face issues regarding sleep?
  • Validation Seeking: How often do you look to seek validation from features of social media?
